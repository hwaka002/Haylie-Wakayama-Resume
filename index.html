I also was intrigued by the potential and possibilities that the biological field had towards helping others and advancing our understanding of science as a whole, especially in the sense of doing things for the greater good.

Since I am still young, I have not been able to gain work experience in my desired field, but have been involved in various extracurricular activities or coursework that gifted me with the relevant skills necessary in such situations. Another experience that I truly learned a lot from was tutoring refugee students in Texas over quarantine. Giving back to the community has always been important to me as well. I also enjoyed being able to learn how to effectively explain concepts to younger students, teaching me more about different learning techniques.
